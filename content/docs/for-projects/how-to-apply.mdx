Teams can apply to launch on AttentionPad through a simple onboarding flow.

### **Submit an Application**

Projects begin by completing the application form, providing:

* team details  
* project overview  
* tokenomics  
* pitch deck or whitepaper  
* roadmap and funding needs  
* Business model  
* Incubation and other support services   
* links to documentation and social channels  
    
  

The more complete the submission, the smoother the assessment process.

### **Required Documents**

To proceed with due diligence, projects must provide:

* whitepaper or litepaper  
* tokenomics and distribution model  
* cap table and allocation details  
* SAFT templates  
* founding team KYC/KYB  
* smart contract details (if already deployed)

These documents help us evaluate legitimacy, readiness, and regulatory alignment.
