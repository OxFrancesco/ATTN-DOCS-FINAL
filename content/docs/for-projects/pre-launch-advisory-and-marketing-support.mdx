AttentionPad provides structured guidance to help projects prepare for a successful launch.

### **GTM and Launch Strategy Review**

Review and refinement of:

* go-to-market strategy and positioning  
* user acquisition loops  
* funnel strategy  
* partner outreach  
* launch narrative and messaging

### **Social Media Promotion**

Visibility across:

* AttentionPad channels  
* Attention Company ecosystem accounts  
* strategic amplification via partners

### **Gamified Campaigns**

Support with:

* quests  
* reward mechanics  
* engagement loops  
* pre-launch activation

### **CJN Media Support**

Depending on fit and readiness, projects may benefit from media formats such as:

* AMAs  
* founder spotlights  
* countdown shows  
* Panellist discussions

### **Strategic Partnerships**

When applicable, teams receive introductions to:

* tooling providers  
* partnered launchpads  
* legal and compliance providers  
* KOL and media outlets networks  
* liquidity providers and market makers  
* advisors and exchange ecosystem actors
