### **Project Token Audits**

AttentionPad requires every project launching through the platform to submit a third-party audit of their token contract or to undergo an audit coordinated through our partners. These audits typically include:

* token standard compliance  
* mint/burn logic and permissions  
* supply controls  
* blacklist/whitelist functions  
* upgradeability risks  
* ownership and admin roles

Audit reports for each individual token sale will be listed on their respective launch pages.

### **Ongoing Security Practices**

In addition to third-party audits, AttentionPad employs several internal and ecosystem-level security practices:

* Automated static analysis and linting during development  
* Deployment verification and bytecode consistency checks  
* Continuous monitoring of token supply and admin-role changes  
* Pause mechanisms for core contracts  
* Bug reporting channels for ethical researchers

These measures complement the audit process and ensure long-term resilience of the platform.
