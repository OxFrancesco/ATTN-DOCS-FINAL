### **Role in the Ecosystem**

$ATTN is the omni-utility token that powers the entire Attention Ecosystem. It acts as a unifying asset across AttentionPad, AttentionEx, Attention, Attention Accelerator, Attention Fund, and Attention Deal Desk, enabling access, participation, rewards, and long-term alignment between users, founders, and ecosystem partners.

Through staking, rewards, governance input, and integrations across ecosystem products, $ATTN becomes the primary mechanism that connects user activity with platform growth.

### **Utility Overview**

Across the ecosystem, $ATTN enables:

* **Staking:** unlock contribution tiers, higher participation limits, partner launchpad access, and enhanced ecosystem rewards.  
* **Token Sale Participation:** staked $ATTN determines contribution caps and unlocks expanded access window tiers.  
* **Rewards:** distributed weekly based on staking, engagement, liquidity, and ecosystem quests.  
* **Burning Mechanisms:** $ATTN may be bought back and burned through ecosystem Free Cash Flow.  
* **Exchange Utility:** fee reductions, premium features, and product-specific perks within AttentionEx.  
* **Governance Input:** advisory voting and sentiment-driven participation across ecosystem decisions.  
* **Liquidity Incentives:** promotional rewards for contributing liquidity to designated pools.

These utilities evolve as additional ecosystem components come online.

### **Supply & Emissions**

**Total Supply:** 5,000,000,000 $ATTN

**Chain:** BNB Smart Chain

**Emission Model:** controlled release through buybacks across staking incentives, burning community rewards, ecosystem operations, partnerships, and liquidity support.

All emissions are designed to align long-term participation with ecosystem growth and avoid unnecessary inflation. See more in the Valueflow chart section.
