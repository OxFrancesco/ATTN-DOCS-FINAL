## **Partnered Sales vs. Community Sales**

* **Partnered Sales:**  
   Projects sourced and vetted by the Attention ecosystem, partners, or collaborators (accelerator, media, founders network).

* **Community Sales:**  
   Projects selected, reviewed, or boosted by the community, including those influenced by the Community Intelligence Engine.
