# **Create Your Account**

To start your journey with AttentionPad, you'll need to create an account.

1.  **Sign-in with Privy**: We use Privy for secure and seamless authentication.
2.  **Social Login**: You can use your X (Twitter) or Google account.
3.  **Wallet Connect**: Connect your preferred Web3 wallet (Metamask, Rabby, etc.).
4.  **Security**: Always ensure you are on the official `attentionpad.com` domain.

# **Complete KYC Verification**

Compliance is key to a safe ecosystem.

*   **Why KYC is required**: To ensure regulatory compliance and prevent bad actors.
*   **Step-by-step flow**: Follow the on-screen instructions after logging in.
*   **Providers**: We partner with **ShuftyPro** for secure identity verification.
*   **Troubleshooting**: If you encounter issues, ensure your documents are clear and valid. Contact support if problems persist.

# **Funding Your Account**

You can fund your wallet to participate in sales using various methods.

*   **Fiat On-Ramp**: Use **Stripe** or **Transak** to buy crypto directly with fiat currency.
*   **Cross-chain Swaps**: Our integrated swap feature allows you to use assets from various chains.
*   **Supported Chains**: We support major EVM chains and L2s for token sales and distribution. (Specific chains will be listed in the dashboard).

# **Understanding Token Sales**

*   **How sales work**: Projects list their tokens for a limited time.
*   **Allocations**: Depending on your tier and the sale type, you will have a minimum and maximum allocation.
*   **Sale Types**:
    *   **FCFS (First Come First Serve)**: Be quick!
    *   **Pro-Rata**: Allocation based on your share of the pool.
    *   **Reserved Tiers**: Guaranteed allocation for high-tier stakers.
*   **Refund Policy**: Some sales include a refund window if the project does not meet specific criteria.
*   **Claiming**: After the sale and TGE (Token Generation Event), claim your tokens via the dashboard according to the vesting schedule.

# **Tracking Your Portfolio**

*   **Dashboard**: View all your active and past participations.
*   **Allocation History**: detailed record of your contributions.
*   **Vesting Schedule**: Track when your tokens unlock.
*   **Claim Interface**: Easy-to-use interface to claim unlocked tokens.

# **Refund Policy**

*   **How it works**: If a refund is triggered (e.g., soft cap not met, or specific protection clause), funds are returned to your wallet.
*   **When refunds apply**: Details are specific to each sale's terms.
*   **Refund Windows**: Typically open for a set period after the sale concludes or if a cancellation event occurs.
