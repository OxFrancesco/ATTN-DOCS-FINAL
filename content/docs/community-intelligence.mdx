# **What Is It?**

The **Community Intelligence Engine** is a crowdsourced due diligence layer. It leverages the collective wisdom of the community to evaluate projects, identify red flags, and highlight hidden gems.

# **How to Participate**

*   **Requirements**: You may need a minimum "Reputation Score" or $ATTN stake to become a Reviewer.
*   **Review Quests**: Specific tasks assigned to reviewers (e.g., "Analyze the whitepaper", "Test the testnet", "Review social sentiment").
*   **Evaluation Dimensions**:
    *   Team Credibility
    *   Product Viability
    *   Tokenomics Health
    *   Community Strength

# **Sentiment Scoring**

*   **Calculation**: Aggregates scores from individual reviewers, weighted by their reputation.
*   **Impact**:
    *   **High Sentiment**: Project may receive "Community Choice" badges, better placement, or marketing boosts.
    *   **Low Sentiment**: Feedback is provided to the project for improvement; severe issues may flag the project for deeper internal review.

# **Leaderboards**

*   **Reviewer Ranking**: Top reviewers are displayed on a leaderboard.
*   **Rewards**:
    *   Earn **Attention Points**.
    *   Earn **$ATTN** (for high-quality, verified insights).
    *   Gain **Reputation** (unlocks higher-tier review tasks).
