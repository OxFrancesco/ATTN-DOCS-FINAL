AttentionPad integrates multiple layers of protection to reduce risk and increase confidence.

# **Compliance Badges (Coming Soon)**

Visual indicators showing that a project has passed specific checks (KYC, KYB, Audits).

# **Contract Deployment & Monitoring (Coming Soon)**

*   **Automated Deployment**: Using AttentionPad's verified factories to ensure contract safety.
*   **Monitoring**: Real-time tracking of token movements and contract interactions post-launch.

# **Anti-Rug & Anti-Dump Measures (Coming Soon)**

*   **Liquidity Locking**: Enforcing liquidity locks automatically via smart contracts.
*   **Vesting Enforcement**: On-chain vesting schedules that cannot be altered by the project owners.

# **Anti-Bots Protection (Coming Soon)**

*   **Mechanisms**:
    *   Captcha verification.
    *   Wallet reputation analysis.
    *   Transaction pattern monitoring.
    *   "Trap" contracts to identify bot behavior.
*   **Goal**: Ensure real humans get allocations, not scripts.
